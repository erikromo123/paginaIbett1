CAST

CAST is a software quality assessment and performance measurement solution that analyzes and measures the structural quality, complexity, and size of software applications. Using highly-sophisticated language analyzers and almost a thousand industry best practice rules for building software, CAST AIP identifies the quality lapses in an application and provides precise guidance on how to fix the problems to IT executives and their teams. It reads and analyzes, by semantically including scripting and interface languages, Web and mainframe technologies, across all tiers of a complex application at the source code level and measures adherence to architectural and coding standards.

How CAST works

CAST Application Intelligence (AI) Platform generates the visibility managers need to measure, control and improve application technical quality and development team performance. Up to date, objective metrics enable executives to better manage and support development teams, whether outsourced, internal or both.
Proactive quality management at the code and architecture level delivers immediate ROI by exposing defects - early in the development lifecycle - that testing cannot find. Most importantly, IT managers deliver value to the company by accelerating time to market, managing IT system risks, improving responsiveness to changing business needs, and controlling maintenance costs over time.


Depth of Analysis

Structural analysis allows CAST to provide timely and accurate metrics that management can rely on when making decisions.

A 10-50% reduction in source code defects early in the development cycle. Early remediation means lower cost, and more satisfied users.

Drill-Down Capability

Not just passive monitoring, but the ability for managers to actively drill down and share technical information that ensures speedy delivery and remediation. Managers can take control and pro-actively improve project outcomes.

CAST platform

CAST Highlight: Cast Highlight is the most comprehensive application portfolio health monitoring solution to increase your software IQ. CAST Highlight empowers IT leaders to turn technical health insights into action, so they can ensure application excellence and peak performance.
CAST Highlight includes :
Application Portfolio Analysis: Essential companion for decision makers. Set up to analyze the health of applications before thy are acquired nd engineered.
Cloud Readiness: Objective evaluation of the application eligibility to move to the cloud.
Agile Analytics; Companies depend on Agile Mojo to predict and improve the agility of sotware across their entire product portfolio.
CAST Appmarq: Appmarq is by far the biggest repository of data about real IT systems. It's built on thousands of analyzed applications, mde of 35 different technologies, by over 300 business organizations across major verticals It provides IT Leaders with factual key analytics to let them know if their applications are on track.
Appmarq is the industry's most extensive application quality benchmarking repository. It contains software quality statistics, trend data and best practices collected from a number of industry sectors and geographies.
CAST Application Analytics Dashboard (AAD): Provides CIO and IT executives with accurate and business relevant analytics bout their applications through a comprehensive dashboard that helps align IT with the business and finance. CAST delivers these insights through Health Factors and Measurements that give real data about your applications. This industry-standard data based oon CISQ, OMG and CWE standards is displayedthrough the AAD in a way that lets you drill-down and explore the metrics that drive your applications.
CAST Application Engineering Dashboard (AED): Lets you examine your code across components, enabling direct insight into your "black box" to pinpointthe exact defects impacting your uptime and performance. CAST AED is the most capable risk detection machine available for IT software. Only the CAST AED provides the ability to truly evaluate structural defects in your code to mitigate RISK before it goes into production. CAST AED provides developers with fast feedbck and guidance to help remove defects early, as well as helping new developers gain a systemlevel vision of the application to produce better software.
CAST Enlighten:CAST Enlighten gives you the power to understand your application architecture and create a visual representation that can be accessed by developers seeking to understand the application.
Enlighten builds this knowledge base by leveraging CAST's renowned structural static code analysis engine the Application Intelligence Platform, to look at how software components interact and then describes these interactions. A visual representation is then created that provides insight to new developers or new team members that helps them better understand, support and continue to develop the application.
