SharePoint

SharePoint Online Office 365
SharePoint is a mobile and intelligent intranet who allows to share and manage content, knowledge and applications to strengthen the work in team, to look for information and to collaborate in the organization.

HOW SHAREPOINT WORKS

SharePoint is born of the need to store the information in an easy and secure way, and at the same time, this available for shared throughout the organization. SharePoint drives teamwork with dynamic and productive group sites for each project, department, and division team. Share files, data, news and resources. Personalize your site to simplify your team's work. Collaborate effortlessly and securely on PC and MAC computers or on mobile devices with team members who are part of your organization or are oblivious to it.


Simple sharing and seamless collaboration

Empower sharing and collaboration on your intranet. Give people places where they can organize and collaborate on content, data, and news to stay on the same page.

Inform and connect your organization

Inform and connect your people with intranets and portals. Share common resources and applications and broadcast your message with rich, dynamic pages.

Intelligence and insights

Rely on powerful enterprise search to find files, sites, and people. Built-in intelligence delivers the most relevant results and helps you discover information and expertise around you.






Mobile apps

Access intranets, team sites and content with the SharePoint mobile app for Android™, iOS®, and Windows and OneDrive mobile apps for Android, iOS and Windows.

Intranets

Inform and engage your organization with intranets and sites to tell your story, announce your news, share resources, streamline processes, and engage people.

Benefits

File sharing and storage: File sharing and at least 1 TB of OneDrive storage per user.
External sharing: Securely share files and content with people inside and outside your organization.
Content management: Organize and manage content in libraries and lists with metadata, records management, and retention policies.
Team sites:Provide a place for your team to organize and collaborate on content, data, and news to stay on the same page.
Discovery: Discover relevant people and important content when you need it most.
DLP capabilities: Use advanced data-loss prevention (DLP) capabilities to identify, monitor, and protect sensitive information.
eDiscovery:Find content in electronic format for litigation or audit scenarios.
Search: Customize your enterprise search and results with enhanced features to surface resources across Office 365.
In-Place Hold: Use In-Place Hold to programmatically prevent content deletion or editing.

Transforms business processes with SharePoint

SharePoint helps to transform the processes of the company to accelerate productivity: from simple tasks such as notifications and approvals to complex operational workflows.

With automation, leading organizations are transferring repetitive functional and cognitive processes to With SharePoint lists and libraries, Microsoft Flow and PowerApps, you can create digital experiences enriched with forms, workflows and custom applications for each site.
